About Us

Our Story

At thinkB, one of Bangalore's leading branding agencies, our journey has been nothing short of inspiring. It all began when our founder decided to break free from a monotonous routine and pursue a creative venture of his own.

What started as Red Advertising, a small design agency, has now evolved into a full-fledged design & digital agency. While times have changed and we’ve grown over the years, our passion for creativity and innovation has remained unwavering.

Meet Our Founder

Sagar Varma’s journey in advertising began in Mumbai, where he gained invaluable experience in the fast-paced world of leading ad agencies. Fuelled by a passion for creativity, he returned to Bengaluru to carve his own path, founding Red Advertising, now known as thinkB Creative Agency. His mission was clear - to break away from traditional corporate moulds and redefine branding with fresh ideas.

Over the years, Sagar has worked with renowned brands like Panasonic, Mahindra, Renault, and Sakra World Hospital. His knack for combining strategy with creativity has helped businesses build impactful campaigns and stand out in competitive markets.

Known for his hands-on approach, Sagar is often found brainstorming with his team or immersed in discussions. His ability to balance big ideas with attention to detail has made thinkB a trusted name in branding. Sagar’s passion continues to drive thinkB’s success in delivering innovative and meaningful campaigns.
